WEDDING DETAILS
The wedding ceremony and reception will be held at Sprucewood Shores Estates Winery in Amherstburg, Ontario. The ceremony will start at 4:30pm. Cocktails and reception to follow.

DIRECTIONS BY AIR
The nearest international airport is in Detroit, Michigan (DTW). You will need a valid passport to cross the Canadian border. Windsor & Amherstburg can be accessed via the Ambassador Bridge or the Detroit-Windsor tunnel.

DIRECTIONS BY CAR
Windsor, Ontario is located off Exit 5 and Amherstburg is located off Exit 10 on Highway 401 (MacDonald-Cartier Freeway).

DIRECTIONS BY TRAIN
Via Rail Canada has daily trains to Windsor along the Quebec City-Windsor corridor.
